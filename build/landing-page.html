How it works

Here are the step-by-step instructions for booking a laundry service with WashUp Laundry

1. Start by scheduling a pickup for your laundry and provide necessary information.
2. Wait for the deliveryman to arrive at the scheduled time and pick up your clothes.
3. After weighing your clothes, we will send a proof of kilo regarding on your laundry.
4. Our staff will then proceed with the laundry cleaning service.
5. Once your laundry is cleaned, we will deliver your clothes to the address you provided.
6. Payment can only be made using the cash on delivery method.
